Testing. Breaking. Re-engineering.

I find the problems of software quality analysis and improvement more interesting than those of software production. Testing mindset is oriented towards breaking things, rather than building new things. How do I know my work is good enough? What does good mean?

That's why I got into SQA. Today, I work with project teams to help them plan Quality Assurance, change control and testing processes. I also assist in product risk analysis, test design and implementation of popular testing techniques.
